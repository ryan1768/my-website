How to Find the Perfect Mentor

Discover the key steps to selecting the right mentor for your success on OnNetwork.

1. Set Your Goals

Before searching for a mentor, identify what you need help with:

  • Are you preparing for college applications, scholarships, or standardized tests?
  • Do you need specialized help (e.g., essay writing, major selection)?
  • Are you looking for long-term guidance or short-term solutions?

2. Use Filters to Narrow Your Search

Refine your mentor list using filters on OnNetwork:

  • School, major, and area of expertise
  • Pricing and availability
  • Past reviews and ratings

3. Explore Mentor Profiles

Learn more about each mentor before making a decision:

  • Read their bio, education, and qualifications
  • Check reviews from other students
  • Watch short introduction videos to see their style

4. Watch Mentor Videos

See mentors in action by watching their introduction videos:

  • Observe their communication style
  • See if their approach fits your learning style
  • Gain confidence in your choice before booking

5. Book a Lesson

Once you find the perfect mentor, schedule your first session:

  • Choose a mentor that aligns with your goals
  • Pick a time that fits your schedule
  • Start learning and achieving your goals!
